The Adoption Journey

1

Initial Inquiry

Reach out to an adoption agency or government body to express your interest. Attend informational meetings to understand the process better.

2

Submit Application

Complete an application form that provides details about your family, financial stability, and reasons for adopting.

3

Home Study

A social worker will conduct interviews, inspect your home, and evaluate your readiness to provide a safe and loving environment for the child.

4

Parent Training

Participate in mandatory training programs to prepare for parenting and learn about challenges associated with adoption.

5

Matching with a Child

Review profiles of children eligible for adoption. Once a match is identified, proceed with the next steps.

6

Legal Formalities

Submit necessary legal documents, attend court hearings, and finalize the adoption process legally.

7

Placement

The child is placed with your family. Follow-up visits by social workers ensure that the child is adjusting well.

8

Post-Adoption Support

Access counseling, support groups, and other resources to help your family navigate life after adoption.
